Privacy Policy

Gaadi Kadh

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Gaadi Kadh (“the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) handles your information. We respect your privacy and have built the App to work without collecting any personal data. By installing and using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or grant permissions that access your personal data.

  • No personal data: we do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any device identifiers.
  • Local game data only: your progress and preferences (such as the current level and sound settings) are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • No analytics or tracking: the App does not include advertising, analytics, or tracking software.

How We Use Information

Because the App does not collect personal information, we do not use, process, sell, or share any personal data. The locally stored game data is used solely to save your progress and settings on your own device so you can continue playing where you left off. Removing the App from your device deletes this local data.

Third-Party Services (Google Play Services)

The App is distributed through the Google Play Store. When you download or use an app from Google Play, Google may collect and process certain information (for example, for app delivery, crash diagnostics, and aggregate statistics) in accordance with its own policies. This collection is performed by Google, not by us, and is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play data practices

The App itself does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or social media services.

Data Security

Since the App does not transmit your data off your device, there is no transfer of personal information for us to secure on a server. Your game data remains on your device and is protected by your device’s own security features. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, but as we do not collect or store your data, the risk of exposure through the App is minimal.

Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no personal data is collected, no such information is ever requested, stored, or disclosed. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us — though, by design, the App provides no way to do so.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
